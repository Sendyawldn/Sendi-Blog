# Hello World

Welcome to my completely redesigned personal space on the internet. I've rebuilt this blog using **Next.js**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **Framer Motion** to deliver a blazingly fast and visually stunning experience.

## Why this stack?

1. **Performance**: Everything is statically generated (SSG) via Next.js.
2. **Aesthetics**: Glassmorphism and smooth micro-interactions powered by Framer Motion. 
3. **Developer Experience**: Writing in pure MDX locally provides an unmatched workflow.
